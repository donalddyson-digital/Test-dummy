FAQ

Questions we get a lot.

Don't see yours? Send it through the form or give us a call.

How much does a website cost?

Starter websites begin at $500 and full custom multi-page sites run around $1,200. Online stores and larger builds are quoted based on scope. All pricing is posted publicly on our services page so you know what to expect before you call.

How long does it take to build a website?

Most starter sites launch in 1 to 2 weeks. Full custom multi-page builds typically take 3 to 4 weeks from consultation to launch. Timeline depends on how quickly you can get content and photos over to us.

Do you only work with businesses in Metro Detroit?

We focus on Royal Oak, Detroit, and the surrounding Metro Detroit area because local knowledge matters for local SEO. We do take on clients outside the region when the project is a good fit.

Will I own my website?

Yes. You own your domain, your content, and your code. We do not lock clients into proprietary platforms. If you ever want to move, your site goes with you.

What is included in the monthly care plan?

Content edits, image swaps, weekly backups, security and uptime monitoring, monthly performance reports, and priority support. Care plans run $100 to $150 per month depending on scope.

Do you do SEO separately from the website build?

Yes. SEO is a monthly service priced at $200 to $400 per month with a 3-month minimum commitment. SEO takes time to move the needle on Google, so anything shorter is wasted money.
