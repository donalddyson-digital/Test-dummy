FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a website take to build?

Most Starter Websites go live within 1–2 weeks of receiving your 50% deposit. Full Custom Sites typically take 2–4 weeks depending on scope, content readiness, and revisions.

Do I need to pay the full amount upfront?

No. A 50% non-refundable deposit is required to start. The remaining 50% is due at launch. The deposit is non-refundable once development begins.

What if I don't like the design?

Every project includes unlimited revisions before development begins and one round of revisions after the first draft. If you're still not satisfied with the final design, I'll revise it until you are — or refund your deposit.

Do you offer hosting?

Most sites are hosted on GitHub Pages at no cost. For more advanced builds I can set you up on a paid host — you always own the account. Hosting management is included in the Monthly Care Plan.

Can I update my website myself?

Yes. I can train you to make basic edits, or you can subscribe to the Monthly Care Plan for $125/mo and I'll handle updates for you with same-day priority support.

Do you work with existing websites on WordPress, Wix, or Squarespace?

Yes. I can work with your existing platform — no need to start over. I'll audit your current site, suggest improvements, and handle updates or redesigns as needed.
